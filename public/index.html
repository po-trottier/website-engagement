Our Love Story

P-O and Athena's story begins the way many modern love stories do — with a swipe… and a very delayed response. They matched on Bumble, and P-O sent a message. Athena saw it… and did not reply. For a few days. P-O assumed he had been gently ghosted — until, out of curiosity (or fate), Athena finally answered.

Their first "date" wasn't even really a date. They met as just friends at Athena's favorite restaurant. She was so sure she'd never see P-O again that she paid for the meal herself. Plot twist: she did. They kept hanging out, including one unforgettable adventure to a questionable part of San Jose that left them both slightly concerned about their life choices — but somehow closer.

Fast forward four years: they now own a home together, share a life with two adorable pups (Cali and Tater Tot), and have built something pretty amazing. Turns out, answering that message was a pretty great decision.
